# Privacy Policy for Leftover

**Last updated: April 9, 2026**

## Overview

Leftover ("we", "our", "the app") is a budget tracking application. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## What We Collect

**Account Information:**
- Email address (used for authentication)
- Display name (optional, used for personalization)

**Financial Data You Enter:**
- Income sources and amounts
- Recurring bills and expenses
- Individual expense transactions
- Savings goals and progress

**Technical Data:**
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Anonymous usage analytics

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide budget tracking and financial insights
- To sync your data across your devices
- To personalize your experience (greetings, recommendations)
- To process your subscription

## Data Storage

Your financial data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore), protected by industry-standard encryption. Each user's data is isolated — no user can access another user's data.

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell your data. Ever.**

We share data only with:
- **Google Firebase** — for secure data storage and authentication
- **Apple** — for Sign in with Apple authentication
- **RevenueCat** — for subscription management

We do not share your financial data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties.

## Subscriptions

Leftover offers a subscription service billed through the Apple App Store. Subscription management and billing is handled entirely by Apple. We use RevenueCat to verify subscription status.

## Your Rights

You can:
- **View** all your data within the app
- **Delete** any income, expense, constant, or savings goal at any time
- **Request account deletion** by contacting us at leftoverbudget@gmail.com
- **Export your data** by contacting us at leftoverbudget@gmail.com

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

Leftover is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data requests:
- Email: leftoverbudget@gmail.com

## California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
